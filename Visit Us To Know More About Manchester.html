Manchester Visitor Information

Visitors to Manchester can contact the Manchester Visitor Information Centre located in the city centre to get more information, help and advice on a range of travel and holiday related areas. There are also visitor centres located in the surrounding areas from Manchester. A visit to the centre makes for a good first port of call to the city and can help ensure your trip is a memorable one. The centre has a range of detailed information for tourists to read through, with other services offered including helping with booking of tours and any accommodation needs you may have. The visitor centre provides visitors with up to date information and can help you gain great local knowledge and tips.

The advice and help given is free of charge and is well worth seeking out if you need more information, advice or need to check something with travel experts. It can save you time and worry to get any queries you may have sorted out with the help of travel experts rather than trying to solve all issues yourself that may be confusing or difficult to sort out. The team at the centre can help with discounted tickets to attractions in the area, heritage passes, tours and offer a range of Manchester branded merchandise and souvenirs.

The Manchester Visitor Information Centre is the main tourist information centre that cover Manchester and there is information available on surroundings areas in the North West of England too. The staff offer free advice and help on different aspects of your travel requirements in Manchester from organising and booking your trips and excursions in Manchester to planning your trip to other parts of the North West of England.

The visitor centre is located in Manchester city centre. The address is:
