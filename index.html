What you need to know about Manchester

They have a good history, vibrant culture, and a world class football clubs. Manchester is a city located in northwest of England. Manchester have a population of over 500,000 people, making it the third largest city in UK. This city have produced some legendary bands and musicians, such as Oasis, Noel Gallagher The Smiths and Joy Division. its also home to the famous Manchester Arena, which is where events take places. Football is a big part of Manchester identity. this city have 2 wonderful football clubs, and they are called Manchester United and Manchester City, The rivalry between these two teams is very intense and adds to the excitement of the sports scene Manchester have old and new ones. You can find buildings like Victoria, Manchester Town Hall, John Rylands Library and so many ones out there. if you like going to shopping, Manchester won't disappoint you when it comes to shopping. this city has a variety of shopping districts, such as Trafford Centre and Arndale this two are more popular in Manchester. you can find everything from high-end designer stores to unique boutiques and vintage shops.
