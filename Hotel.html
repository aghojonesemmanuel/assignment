The Midland Hotel

The Midland offers unrivalled levels of luxury, with 312 elegant hotel rooms and suites housed in a coveted location in Manchester’s city centre. Some rooms offer stunning views of the Manchester skyline, and are stylishly outfitted with sumptuous, modern furnishings; the perfect antidote to the building’s historic design.

The Midland experience doesn’t end when you leave your room. The hotel’s award-winning spa is a true sanctuary, where guests can relax and rejuvenate after a long day of travelling. Experience a blissful massage or feel your worries slip away in the heated relaxation pool. Why not book a bespoke spa day and unwind with friends while enjoying a combination of special designed treatments? Health-conscious guests can work out in the fully equipped gym or go for a swim in the indoor pool.

At The Midland’s award-winning restaurants, guests can savour world-class cuisine, whether it’s lunch or dinner with family, catching up with friends during afternoon tea in the dedicated tea room, or having cocktails and canapés in the hotel’s chic Midland Bar.

When you stay at The Midland, all the attractions of Manchester are right on your doorstep. Catch a show at the Manchester Opera House, or buy gifts for family back home in the Market Street shopping district. Conveniently located in the heart of Manchester, The Midland is also the ideal place for hosting events, conferences and events. The Midland’s 14 elegant event venues offer everything you need for a bespoke, memorable event, whether it’s a professional meeting or a fairytale wedding. If you’re booking a holiday in Manchester, The Midland Manchester will ensure you have a stylish, sophisticated stay.

Hotel Football Old Trafford

Hotel Football Old Trafford is a unique hotel that caters to football fans. It's located near Old Trafford Stadium, the home of Manchester United. The hotel has a fun and vibrant football-themed atmosphere, with stylish rooms that offer comfort and modern amenities. Guests can enjoy football-inspired decor, memorabilia, and even a rooftop football pitch. It's a great choice for those who want to immerse themselves in the football culture of Manchester.
